Neocities is special. Here's why I love it:

## The Indie Web Spirit

Neocities carries on the spirit of the old web - when everyone had their own unique homepage. No templates, no algorithms, just creativity.

## Community

The Neocities community is genuinely lovely. People leave nice comments, join webrings, and support each other's sites.

## Freedom

No character limits. No content algorithms. No monetization pressure. Just pure, creative freedom.

## Nostalgia + Modern

You can embrace old-web aesthetics while using modern tools. It's the best of both worlds!

---

If you're thinking about making your own site, do it! The web is better when it's weird and personal.
